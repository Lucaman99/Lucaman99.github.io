Burlington Mathematics Club

About Us


The Burlington Math Club is a group of high school, college, and university students, from all across Burlington and the surrounding areas that share a common interest and fascination for subjects within pure mathematics. The goal of the BMC is to create a network of individuals that can come together to talk about concepts in math, and interesting ideas across a wide range of mathematical disciplines. The Burlington Math Club will schedule regular meetings where members can come together to discuss mathematics, learn about new ideas, or solve problems together. The BMC will also have lectures, given by members of the club, creating the opportunity to educate others about a certain part of mathematics. The BMC also hopes to have guest speakers, problem solving sessions, and other interesting, math-related meetings and discussions. Outside of our meetings, the BMC will maintain a Slack group, where members can discuss mathematical topics with others at any time, across a wide range of subject areas.

We ultimately hope that we at the Burlington Math Club can create a forum for enlightened and interesting mathematical conversations, as well as create a medium where ideas can be shared and everyone can learn something new about mathematics.
