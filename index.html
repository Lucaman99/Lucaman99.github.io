About

In the Fall of 2025, I will be starting my PhD at the University of Chicago. Prior to this, I was an undergraduate student at the University of Toronto.

My research interests are (very broadly) related to differential and algebraic topology/geometry and mathematical physics. Much of my undergraduate research was in quantum algorithms and quantum information, but this is no longer my main focus.
