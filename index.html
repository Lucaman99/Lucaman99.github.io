About

I am currently a fourth-year pure mathematics student at the University of Toronto.

My research interests are broadly related to symplectic and Riemannian geometry, mathematical physics, algebraic topology, K-theory, and discrete complex analysis/geometry. Much of my previous research has been in the fields of quantum algorithms and quantum information, and I continue to be very interested in using powerful techniques from mathematics to address questions in these fields.
