Inferno

A Python Library for the Simulation of Land Wildfires

Inferno is an open-source Python library with the goal of helping researchers specializing in fields ranging from climate science to mathematics to simulat the evolution of land wildfires with computational models. We are currently in the process of overhauling our entire library to vastly expand functionality, as well as writing a whitepaper documenting the ideas that are being used to develop the library.
