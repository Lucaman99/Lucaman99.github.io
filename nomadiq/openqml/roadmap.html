OpenQML Project Roadmap



Phase I

Over the course of the next 4 months, our plans are to continue expanding the OpenQML gallery. We hope to introduce Notebooks on the following topics to the gallery:

  • Proof-of-Concept Protein Folding With QAOA
  • Basic Quantum Cellular Automata
  • Many-Body Quantum Simulations with Different Ansatzes
  • Quantum Hamiltonian Based Models
  • Foundational Quantum Machine Learning (i.e. Baqprop, Quantum Natural Gradient Descent, etc.)

In addition to this, we are in the process of making modifications to our pre-existing Notebooks, in order to make them even more comprehensive and accessible. Finally, we are also hoping to scale the project by accepting contributions from the quantum open-source community, to make our gallery even more diverse by drawing upon the unique skills of many different individuals. We also hop to "translate" our new Notebooks and our existing Notebooks to a variety of the different quantum programming languages (Cirq, Qiskit, Pennylane, etc.).
