OpenQML Project Roadmap



In the coming months, our plans are to continue expanding the OpenQML gallery. Over the course of the next 6 months, we hope to introduce Notebooks on the following topics to the gallery:

  • Proof-of-Concept Protein Folding With QAOA
  • Basic Quantum Cellular Automata
  • Many-Body Quantum Simulations with Different Ansatzes
  • Quantum Hamiltonian Based Models
  • Foundational Quantum Machine Learning

In addition to this, we are in the process of making modifications to our pre-existing Notebooks, in order to make them even more comprehensive and accessible. Finally, we are also hoping to scale the project by accepting contributions from the quantum open-source community, to make our gallery even more diverse by drawing upon the unique skills of many different individuals.
