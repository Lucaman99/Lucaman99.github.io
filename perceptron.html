Simulating Quantum Algorithms With Cirq

The Creation of a Perceptron Implemented Into a Quantum Circuit

The goal of this project was to create a percpetron that could be used within a quantum circuit, and ultimately implemented into neural networks and other computational models. I used the Google Cirq framework to test my algorithm, and I was able to create a mathematical explanation that proved the algorithm works under all circumstances
