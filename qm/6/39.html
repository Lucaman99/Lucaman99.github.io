We are asked to consider a model of perturbations in a crystal lattice. We begin with the Hamiltonian that describes a hydrogen atom, and then we perturb it in the following way: we place three pairs of point charges at equal radius around the hydrogen atom (which are supposed to model the protons in neighbouring atoms, in a hexagonal lattice of hydrogen atoms). In order to do this, we must understand how these pertrubations will interact with the electron, and how they will interact with the proton. Let's start by considering proton-proton interactions. We let the distance from the central proton to the $i$-th neighbouring proton to be $d_i$, and the charge to be $q_i$.
